# Mara Okonkwo — Profile

## Background
Mara Okonkwo is a robotics engineer based in Lisbon, Portugal. She grew up in
Enugu, Nigeria, and moved to Portugal in 2017. She holds a master's degree in
Mechatronics from the University of Porto, which she completed in 2016.

## Contact and Preferences
Mara prefers to be contacted by email in the mornings; she reserves afternoons
for deep work and does not take meetings after 3pm. Her working language is
English, though she is fluent in Igbo and conversational in Portuguese.

## Dietary Notes
Mara is lactose intolerant and follows a pescatarian diet. She is allergic to
peanuts, so any recipe or restaurant suggestion must avoid them entirely.

## Health and Fitness
Mara runs three times a week and is training for the Lisbon half-marathon in
October. Her resting heart rate is 54 bpm. She sees a physiotherapist every
second Friday for a recurring left-knee issue.
